To balance my parenting and learning, I already have some plans for Foundations and Bootcamp. I aim to study no less than 40 hours per week and spare a weekend day to enjoy my family. For the Bootcamp, I've arranged with my partner to cover the daily daycare drop-off and pick-up so I could work uninterrupted for longer. Communication is vital to working productively with others. I plan to keep my team and facilitator informed of my schedule and use the appropriate discord status when logged in. Regarding what I plan to do when things get complicated, I plan to take a step back, have a break, and consider possible solutions. I'll assess if I can solve the problem on my own or if I need help from others. I'll reach out to my facilitator and have a talk about it as I'm sure they've been through something similar or have helped someone with the same. I'm also planning to care about my well-being by exercising. Last year I suffered from postpartum depression, and exercising was vital in my recovery, so I want to keep myself as active as possible.
